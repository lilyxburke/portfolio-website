Experience

Dlala Studios (QA tester)

June 2024

I undertook a week's placement at Dlala studios as part of the QA department, where I was tasked with functional testing, ensuring that projects ran as intended and keeping track of bugs using Jira. I tested previous bugs to see if they had been fixed and also discovered new bugs that I then had to replicate for a report. I also sat in on team meetings and took part in discussions that helped me to further understand how a games studio operates and how different departments communicate.

I also spoke to part of the technical team and was given an insight into the different stages of game development as well as the different tools and packages that can be used to optimise elements of Unity gameplay.
