Potion time

For my second project, I wanted to do something on a larger scale and decided on a task-based game. I chose this type of game as it felt very modular, making it easier to plan out the separate minigames. Below are photos of my current minigames that I am working on. (All artwork is not final!). In the future, I hope to add difficulty levels to the game and also make it multiplayer if possible.

In this minigame, you must pet the cat in the correct order at the correct body part based on certain features such as the fur colour and its mood etc.



In the rune task, you must calculate the power level of a spell based on the runes given. Each symbol (currently represented by letters) and shape has an effect on the overall level.



Here is where all the tasks are shown, you are able to dip in and out of each of them, keeping progress saved in between. There is also a manual which can be accessed by pressing the white arrow on the right that will have all infomration needed to complete each puzzle.
